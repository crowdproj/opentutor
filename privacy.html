Privacy Policy

This application uses Keycloak as its identity provider to authenticate users and manage secure access to app features.

As part of the authentication process, Keycloak may store and manage personal information such as the user's name and email address. This information is maintained within the Keycloak identity server and is not directly accessed, collected, or processed by the application itself, except for the technical user ID (subject identifier) required for session management.

The application does not store or share personal data with third parties, nor does it use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

All authentication and access control operations are performed securely using self-hosted infrastructure controlled by the application developer.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your data or privacy, please contact us at ipfaboslgd@gmail.com.
