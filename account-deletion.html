Account Deletion Request

If you have created an account in the OpenTutor app and would like to request its deletion, please contact us at ipfaboslgd@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

We will manually process your request and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

What data will be deleted:

  • Your account identity (user ID)
  • Your personal learning progress (if available)
  • Your custom dictionaries and flashcards (if stored server-side)

What data may be retained: None, unless required by law. All deletions are permanent and handled manually on request.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out.
